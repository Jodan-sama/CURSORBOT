## Why the UI shows "Up 4c"

We are **not** buying 4c. The bot always places at **96c, 97c, or 99c** (B1c/B2c/B3c).

- We bought the **Down** outcome at **96c** (spread was negative, so strategy chose "no" = Down).
- In a binary market, Down at 96c implies the other side (Up) trades at ~4c (100 − 96).
- Polymarket’s History can show the **other** outcome’s price (4c) next to the outcome name, so you see "Up 4c" even though we bought **Down at 96c**.

So: **we bought Down 96c**. The "Up 4c" in the UI is the complementary outcome’s price, not our fill price. Nothing changed with the restart; the strategy and placement logic are correct.
